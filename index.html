On what day of the week were you born?

You probably already know the date you were born but do you know the exact day of the week you were born? Like most of us, you probably don't. Lucky for you, I can help with that. All you need to do is fill the form below and I will calculate the day you were born. On top of that, I'm going to give you a new Akan name based on your gender. Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture, where most of the time children are named after the day of the week they are born.
